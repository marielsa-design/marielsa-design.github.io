Established 1924

The soul of Heritage care

For a century, Open Bullet has defined the intersection of botanical wisdom and artisanal dedication. We believe that what goes into your pet's life should be as pure as the bond you share.


A Century of Curation

Founded in the heart of the countryside, Open Bullet began as a small apothecary specializing in traditional remedies for working companions. Our founder, Arthur Sterling, believed that nature held every answer if one only knew where to look. Today, we maintain that same devotion to the source. Our ingredients are harvested with the seasons, respecting the natural rhythm of the Earth. We don't just manufacture supplies; we curate a lifestyle of longevity and grace.
